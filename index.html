Polyphonic Interlace is a symphonious, surround-sound performance conceived and composed by Raquel Acevedo Klein that will be performed at Little Island on August 29th at 12pm and September 5th at 12pm. The music invites you to travel amidst a sea of voices, emerging from several directions. Raquel will provide a countdown and invite the audience to play parts of the piece from their phones aloud as they walk around The Glade.

Each track has been pre-recorded by composer and vocalist Raquel Acevedo Klein, who weaves her own voice into a polyphonic tapestry of stories from across New York City at the cusp of reopening. All are welcome to play parts of the music from a smartphone.

Location
Polyphonic Interlace will take place at the Glade on the Little Island:
a 2.4-acre public park in Hudson River Park on the West side of Manhattan. It is located in the Hudson River off the West Side Highway with entrances at West 13th and 14th Streets.
